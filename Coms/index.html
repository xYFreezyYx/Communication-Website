UpWorked

Information

The site is for students to exchange files and get help for tasks.
Teachers can get feedback on the tasks of other teachers, analyze how students see tasks and their thinking process.



Sites Purpose

The students can posts documents and tasks that teachers have sent them.
Together they can analyse and get help with the task.
Teachers can see how students think and how they interpret the subject.
Know how to better write assignments that students can't understand.
The site will be like a forum that is special for each subject and it's going to be like chat or discussion board.
Logging in helps to keep track of your friends posts and even help them with tasks and get the replies and help if they post the same thing as you.
